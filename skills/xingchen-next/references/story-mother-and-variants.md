# Story Mother And Variants

## Story Mother

`StoryMother` is the single approved content mother for a project.

It should carry:

- one thesis
- one audience promise
- one scene spine
- one proof binding map
- one narration spine
- one visual intent summary

It is the source for every platform variant.

## Platform Variants

Each `PlatformVariant` should stay traceable to the mother.

Allowed changes:

- different hook ordering
- different pacing density
- different subtitle placement policy
- different title and cover options
- small scene packaging shifts

Not allowed without explicit approval:

- thesis rewrite
- proof meaning changes
- replacing the whole scene order
- switching the visual family away from the approved mother intent

## Default Variant Targets

Recommended first-wave variants:

- `douyin`
- `xiaohongshu`
- `wechat-channels`
- `bilibili`

The same mother may serve all four, but each platform may need different openers and delivery density.

## Mother First Rule

- derive variants from one approved mother
- do not branch early just because one platform prefers a different rhythm
- if a platform truly needs a different argument, create a new mother with a new approval trace
