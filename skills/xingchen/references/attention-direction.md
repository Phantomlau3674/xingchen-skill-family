# Attention Direction

Xingchen V2 optimizes for effect, not for maximal noise.

## Principle

Give the scene exactly enough spectacle to make the point land, and exactly enough clarity to make it believable.

## Decision Rule

- if trust is the bottleneck, clarity wins
- if memory is the bottleneck, a stronger anchor wins
- if emotion is the bottleneck, controlled spectacle wins
- if proof is pixel-critical, readability wins over style

## By Scene Job

- `prove`: clarity first, style second
- `announce`: speed and impact first, readability close behind
- `contrast`: tension and hierarchy first
- `explain`: sequencing first, spectacle restrained
- `release`: compression and payoff first

## Practical Rule

Do not ask "should this be clear or cool?"

Ask:

- what must be immediately understood
- what may be felt before it is fully parsed
- what must still be readable at video speed
